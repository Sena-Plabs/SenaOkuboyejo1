Designing Human-Centered Solutions Through Data & Strategy

Where strategic thinking meets human-centered design. As an Assistant Professor and Certified Scrum Product Owner, I bridge the gap between complex data insights and meaningful human experiences through thoughtful design and strategic implementation.

🎯 Teaching Innovation: Developing critical thinking through investigative data analysis and collaborative team-based projects, empowering students to uncover insights, craft compelling narratives, and master analytical problem-solving skills. Students build confidence through in-class presentations and showcase their work at student conferences, bridging technical expertise with professional communication skills.

🚀 AI Workflow Architecture: Designing and implementing intelligent workflows that enhance human capabilities while maintaining focus on user experience and accessibility.

💡 Strategic Product Leadership: CSPO-certified professional who combines agile methodologies with human-centered design principles to create impactful educational technology solutions.

I design solutions that put humans at the center of technological innovation, creating meaningful connections between data insights and real-world impact.
