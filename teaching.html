Teaching Excellence

Innovative curriculum development with equity-centered pedagogy

Overview

My teaching philosophy centers on equity-centered pedagogy, AI integration in MIS courses, and fostering critical thinking through innovative projects like the acclaimed "Data Detectives" initiative.

Key Initiatives

Data Detectives Project: An innovative curriculum component that engages students in real-world data storytelling, developing both technical skills and critical thinking abilities.

AI Integration: Seamlessly incorporating artificial intelligence tools and concepts into Management Information Systems coursework to prepare students for the future of work.

Equity-Centered Approach: Designing inclusive learning experiences that acknowledge diverse backgrounds and learning styles while promoting equal access to educational opportunities.

Impact

Through these innovative teaching methods, students develop not only technical competencies but also the critical thinking skills necessary to navigate complex data-driven decision making in their future careers.
