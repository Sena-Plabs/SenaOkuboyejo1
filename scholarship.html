Scholarship Overview

My scholarship centers on the intersection of technology, behavior change, and equitable access in higher education and healthcare settings. Research focuses on digital health interventions, AI workflows in education, and equity-centered analytics that prioritize fairness and accessibility in data-driven decision making.
